Cell Analogy: Animal Cell

Cell Membrane

The cell membrane is like airport security because they both restrict what can go in or out and they protect the inside

Mitochondria

The Mitochondria is like the power grid that powers the airport because they both produce power for their bigger part

Ribosomes

The Ribosomes are like the people in the airport becasue the they both supply the things that are used to repair and sustain the airport

Endoplasmic Reticulum

The Endoplasmic Reticulum is like the food court becasue the both supply the cell / airport with the food it needs

Nucleus

The Nucleus is like the control center because they both control what happens inside the cell / airport and makes sure it goes smoothly

Vacuoles

The Vacuoles are like the trash cans because they both collect garbage to dispose of later

Nuclear Envelope

The Nuclear envelope is like the gate security because they are both like a second form of defence for somthing important

Golgi apparatus

The Golgi apparatus is like gate becasue they both synthesize / group up people / proteins to do things

Lysosomes

The Lysosomes are like the garbage people becasue they both dispose of garbage

Centrioles

The Centrioles are like ticket printers becasue they both reprint somthing important to the user
